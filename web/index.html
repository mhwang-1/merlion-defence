How to Play

🎯 Goal

Stop the hantus before they overrun the Temporary Operations Command at the end of the road! Each leaked creature costs lives. Lose all lives and the neighbourhood falls.

🏗 Building

Tap a concrete pad beside the road to build a tower. Tap a tower to upgrade, sell or move its rally point (NS Camp only).

⏩ Waves

Press NEXT WAVE early to earn bonus gold. Use the ⏸ / ⏩ buttons to pause or fast-forward.

🏆 Challenges

Beat a level with ★★★ to unlock its 🔥 Heroic and 🛡 Iron Challenges — elite waves or one long siege, one life only, each worth a bonus ★.

🦸 Heroes

Your hero fights beside your towers — tap the hero, then tap the ground to move them. Acts 1–2 field one hero; Acts 3–4 field two. Fallen heroes respawn after a short wait.

🎖 Merits

Clearing levels and challenges earns merit points. Spend them in the Armory to unlock new towers and legendary heroes, and choose your hero loadout there.

💾 Saving

Progress autosaves in the browser. Use EXPORT SAVE on the menu to download a backup .json, and LOAD SAVE to restore it.

🗼 Towers

📡 Cell-Base Tower — rapid 5G microwave zaps. Hits ground & flying. (physical)

🍈 Durian Launcher — lobs the king of fruits. Splash damage, ground only.

⛩ Talisman Temple — enchanted talismans pierce armour, slow spirits. (magic)

🎖 NS Camp — deploys NSmen who block enemies on the road.

🎯 Mata Sniper Post — slow, long-range shots that ignore armour.

🔥 Hawker Wok — flaming splash that keeps burning. Ground only.

⚡ Substation Coil — magic lightning that chains between spirits.

🍧 Ice Kacang Cart — freezing pulse chills every spirit in range.

👹 Know your enemy

Armoured spirits resist physical hits — use the Temple. Spectral ones resist magic — use durians & 5G. Flying hantus ignore roads and soldiers.
